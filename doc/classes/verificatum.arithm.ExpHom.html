Class ExpHom

Exponentiation homomorphism from a ring to a group. Note that the group is not necessarily a prime order group, that the ring is not necessarily a field, and that the ring is not necessarily the ring of exponents of group.

param basis

Basis element that is exponentiated.

param domain

Domain of homomorphism, which may be a subring of the ring of exponents of the basis element.
